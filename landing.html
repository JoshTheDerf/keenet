End-to-End Encrypted

Your vault is encrypted with AES-256 or ChaCha20. Your master password never leaves your device.

KeePass Compatible

Open and edit .kdbx files from KeePass, KeePassXC, or any compatible client. Full interoperability.

Cross-Platform

Native apps for macOS, Windows, Linux, and Android. Or use the web app from any browser with PWA support.

Works Offline

The web app installs as a PWA and works fully offline. Your data stays on your devices — no cloud required.

Open Source

MIT licensed and fully open source. Audit the code, contribute, or self-host the web app on your own server.

Biometric Unlock

Unlock your vault with Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint on supported mobile and desktop devices.
